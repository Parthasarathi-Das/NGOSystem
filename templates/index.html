How It Works

From crisis to resolution — powered by smart matching

🆘

Team in Crisis Submits Request

Affected teams fill out the crisis form with their location, district, and nature of emergency.

💛

Donor Submits Resources

Donors provide their contact details and what they can contribute — food, medicine, supplies, or money.

🏢

NGO Admin Reviews & Adds Members

The admin sees all requests and maintains the member roster with their skills and district.

⚡

System Auto-Allocates

Members are matched to crises by district & skills. Donor resources are allocated to nearby crises automatically.
